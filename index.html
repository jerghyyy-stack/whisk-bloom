The AVENN menu

Eight collections, one premium feel.

The menu is designed to stay simple behind the scenes: one excellent house cream, layered across indulgent drinks, mint chocolate, refreshing sodas, fruit teas and cheesecakes.

01Cream Top

Matcha, strawberry and sesame drinks finished with house cream.

02Crème Brûlée

Caramel custard-inspired drinks with a rich brûlée finish.

03Cookie

Oreo, Biscoff and chocolate cookie drinks for repeat cravings.

04Mint Collection

Chocolate mint cream drinks with mint chocolate shards and cookie crunch.

05Cream Soda

Bright fruit sodas softened with a smooth cream cap.

06Fruit Tea

Light jasmine and oolong teas with real fruit notes.

07Magic Potion

Limited-edition sparkle drinks for launches and festive drops.

08Desserts

Oreo cheesecake and strawberry matcha cheesecake, available by slice or whole cake.
